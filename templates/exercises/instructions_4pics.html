4 Pics, 1 Word - Instructions

Welcome to the "4 Pics, 1 Word" game! Test your skills by identifying the common word that links four images.

Objective

Identify the single word that connects all four displayed images.

How to Play

  1. View the Images: Examine the four images presented on the screen.
  2. Find the Connection: Determine the word that links all four images.
  3. Enter Your Answer: Type your answer into the input box labeled "Your Answer".
  4. Submit Your Answer: Click the "Submit" button to check if your answer is correct.
  5. Navigate Between Puzzles: Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons to move between puzzles.

Game Rules

  • One Word Only: Your answer must be a single word.
  • Case Insensitivity: Answers are not case-sensitive.
  • Answer All Puzzles: Complete all puzzles in the set to finish the game.

Scoring

Earn one point for each correct answer. Your final score will be displayed at the end of the game.
